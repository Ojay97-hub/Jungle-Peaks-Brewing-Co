Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our tours and tastings

What should I wear for the tour?

We recommend comfortable clothing and closed-toe shoes for safety during the tour. Dress for the weather as parts of our tour may be outdoors.

Can I bring my kids to the tasting session?

Our tasting sessions are limited to guests aged 21 and over. Children are welcome in our taproom area where we offer non-alcoholic beverages and food.

How do I book a tour or tasting experience?

You can book directly through our website using the booking buttons above, or contact our customer service team for assistance and special group rates.

What are your tour times?

Tours run daily from 11 AM to 7 PM, with the last tour starting at 6 PM. Sunset tours are seasonal and times vary based on sunset. Check our booking calendar for availability.
